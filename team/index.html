Leader

Ruihua Song

Dr. Song Ruihua is a highly accomplished researcher and author in the field of artificial intelligence. She received her Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, and her Ph.D. from the Department of Computer Science at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. She joined Microsoft Asia Research Institute in 2003, where she worked on developing novel machine learning algorithms for natural language processing. She has published numerous papers in top-tier conferences and journals, and holds several international patents. She currently works as a tenured associate professor at the Gopher AI Institute at Renmin University of China, where her research focuses on multimodal dialogue systems and multimodal natural language understanding.
